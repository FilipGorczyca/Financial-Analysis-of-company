# Financial-Analysis-of-company
Analysis of AC S.A. financial health using balance sheet, income statement, cash flows, ratios, and strategic insights.

Scope: Vertical and horizontal analysis of assets, equity, liabilities; profitability, liquidity, leverage, efficiency ratios; DuPont ROE decomposition; cash conversion cycle; working capital structure; market indicators; automotive industry benchmarking; SWOT with recommendations.
Years: 2018–2020.
Methodology: Three-year trend and comparative analysis; industry benchmark comparison; operational risk evaluation in liquidity, inventory, leverage; strategic strengths and weaknesses identification.
Key Findings: High profitability above industry average; heavy export dependence; declining inventory efficiency raising cash cycle risk; strong equity and capital stability; ROE drop from lower asset turnover and rising costs; overall stability with working capital improvement needed.
Tools: Excel for calculations and trends; public AC S.A. statements; sector reports and academic databases.
Objective: Assess financial health and performance 2018–2020; identify risks and opportunities for strategic decisions.
